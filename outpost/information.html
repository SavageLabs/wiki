About

OutpostX (a.k.a. SavageFactions) is a feature-packed koth plugin, an essential to every factions server.

Features

  • Factions, Towny, Lands, Parties and Skyblock support
  • Auto-generated Holograms
  • Exp, McMMO and command rewards
  • Damage and ShopGUI+ multiplier rewards 

Download

You can download the latest release of SkyblockX from Spigot (recommended), or our preview builds from SavageLabs' website!
