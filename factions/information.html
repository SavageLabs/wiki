About

FactionsX is a modern implementation of the factions gamemode written from the ground up with extensibility, speed, and usability in mind.

The plugin can currently be obtained through the early access program on patreon: https://patreon.com/ProSavage

Any rank VIP ($5) or higher gives access.

 

FactionsX is modular, in the context that, it supports addons. They can be simply dropped and inside of the addons folder generated inside of the FactionsX datafolder.

Currently FactionsTop is the only addon - it is available on Patreon on the VIP+ tier or higher.
