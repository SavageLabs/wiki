About

SkyblockX is a performance-oriented, modern and fully functional skyblock core with minimal dependencies. 
It was designed because of ASkyblock being discontinued and also not being supported past Spigot-1.12 and all replacements being poorly designed or unstable.

The core features of our plugin are:

  • Optimisation - By running the most intensive tasks in async, we make sure that our plugin is as lightweight as possible.
  • Customizability - It is completely customizable, meaning that you can change everything - from functionality to the content of its messages.
  • Open Source - If you ever question what's inside our skyblock core, check our GitHub Page!

Features

  • Powerful Questing System
  • Island Levels, Worth and Leaderboards
  • Island Teams and Member Management
  • Custom GUIs
  • Nether Islands and many other..!

Upcoming Features

  • Island Upgrades
  • Island Boosters
  • Player and Island Shops
  • End Islands
